AntFit Getting Started

A fit task for Ant

  1. Copy antfit.jar to your $ANT_HOME/lib directory.
  2. Add a taskdef to your build.xml: 
    <taskdef name="fit" classname="com.cmdev.fit.ant.FitTask">
  3. Add a target and call the fit task. 
            <target name="runfit" depends="compile">
            <fit destdir="${fit.results.dir}">
                <fileset dir="${fit.in.dir}">
                    <include name="*.html"/>
                </fileset>
            </fit>
	</target>
  4. Examine the results files in ${fit.results.dir}
